Explore. Share. Embed.

Kompakkt is an open source 3D viewer working towards IIIF compliance.

Kompakkt supports images, videos, audio files and 3D models, including point clouds and meshes. Explore and annotate them in 3D, create you own collections and collaborate within your community. You can store media files on your own infrastructure or embed uploaded objects on other websites via iframe.

Semantic kompakkt is a free and open source extension of Kompakkt allowing the annotation of 3D models, and other visual media, within a linked open data (LOD) environment. Semantic Kompakkt supports data enrichment from global authority files and offers search and querying via user-friendly graphical interfaces and open APIs.
